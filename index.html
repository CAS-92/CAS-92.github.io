ABOUT THE BISTRO

Welcome to The Inkwell, located in the heart of the Baltic, Liverpool, where Chef/Owner William Shuttleworth curates a relaxed yet refined dining experience. At the core of our culinary philosophy is a commitment to using the best seasonal produce, with homemade delights like bread, oils, charcuterie, and more. While not all details are listed on the menu, Chef Shuttleworth is always ready to share insights into our dishes and our network of suppliers. Join us for a celebration of exquisite cuisine and culinary mastery at The Inkwell, where every visit is a journey through the freshest and finest flavors Liverpool has to offer.
