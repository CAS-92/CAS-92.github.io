ABOUT THE CAFE

Dine with us at The Inkwell, where our esteemed chef/owner, William Shuttleworth, has curated an exceptional menu revolving around the richness of seasonal produce. Through his skilled craftsmanship and dedication, he ensures that each dish is a harmonious blend of flavors, textures, and visual appeal. The result is an elevated dining experience that showcases the freshness and quality of locally sourced ingredients, making every visit to our restaurant a celebration of exquisite cuisine and culinary mastery under William's seasoned guidance.
